This booklet is an English translation of the address by Revered Swami Vireswaranandaji, the tenth president of the Ramakrishna Order, to the youth on the first National Youth Day celebrations held at Belur Math on 12 January 1985. The second part of this book contains his benedictory address to the assembled youth on the same occasion. The focus of the booklet is to empower youth to rebuild India on the lines envisaged by Swami Vivekananda. This booklet will help students build goals on the lines of spirituality and service, and make our country great again.
