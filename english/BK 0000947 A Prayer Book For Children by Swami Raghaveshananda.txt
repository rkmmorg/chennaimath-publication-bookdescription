A selection of simple prayers to popular Hindu Gods &amp; Godesses (and Guru) in Sanskrit for children to learn with explanation and prayers. Includes Devanagari text phonetic pronunciation with English letters and English translation.
 
 "May He (the Sumpreme Lord) protect us both (the teacher and the student). May He nourish us both. May our study be thorough and fruitful. May we not hate each other."
