Youth is generally identified with energy, enterprise, strength and optimism. The challenge is how to channel this power into creative and positive directions. This collection of articles by different authors is a guidebook to harness its potential by developing a way of life that carries one through life in a postive and sucessful manner. 
Authors include: Swami Trigunatita, Swami Atmashraddhananda, Swami Gautamananda, Swami Budhananda, Swami Bhaskarananda, Swami Dayatmananda, Swami Brahmeshananda, Swami Nityasthananda, Swami Baneshananda, Swami Bodhamayananda, Pravrajika Akhandaprana and others.
