This collection of articles by renowned monks of Ramakrishna Order is a guidebook for youth to harness their potential power and channelizing that energy into creative and positive directions. Some of the authors include Swami Trigunatita, Swami Budhananda, Swami Gautamananda, Swami Bhaskarananda, Swami Dayatmananda, Swami Brahmeshananda and Swami Nityasthananda.
