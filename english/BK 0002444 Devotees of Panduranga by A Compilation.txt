This book for children is a collection of stories about nine devotees of Lord Panduranga Vittala, a Hindu God predominately worshipped in Maharashtra and Karnataka. The stories of Pandaripur, Chokamela, Bhakta Neelobhaji, Bhakta Kanakadasa, Haridas, Kabirdas, Bhakta Raakha, Sri Jayadeva and the story of Shivaji blessed by Vittala are explained through colourful illustrations. The spiritual kernel is also explained through the teachings of Sri Ramakrishna and Swami Vivekananda at the end of each story.
