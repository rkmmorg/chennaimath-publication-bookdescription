This booklet contains teachings and instructions selected from the letters of Swami Ramakrishnananda which provide the much needed consolation for the struggling spiritual aspirants. Swami Ramakrishnananda is a direct disciple of Sri Ramakrishna. He was a great thinker, forceful speaker, and above all, a God-realized soul. This booklet will also provide the much needed succor and solace to people in distress.
