

This 4 colour illustrated book presents some glimpses of excellence achieved by the ancient Indians in various fields of human endeavour.

The author of this work, Dr. (Mrs.) Suruchi Pande, having doctorates in Medical Ethics and Ethno-Ornithology, through painstaking research has compiled the literary evidences and references to monumental texts that endorse the acclaimed ancient Indian genius in various fields of human endeavour that can baffle even the experts of the today?s world!
